Manoranjan Behura

Director of Commerce Point

It gives me immense pleasure to inform that our institution ’commerce point’ which was established on 15th August 1997 has become a hall mark in the field of commerce education. Initially we had only five students. Now we impart education to more than 500 students each year. Commerce point has produced more than five thousand students who have established themselves in many professions like CA, CMA, and CS and well placed in different corporate sectors with handsome packages. Some of our students are also serving in different colleges after the completion of their higher education [ MBA, MFC and M.com]with attractive remunerations. For the last 10 years the students of commerce point have been obtaining consecutively the topper position of Salepur college. Our students also occupy the first 20 positions of Salepur college-+ in CHSE. Two / Three of our students glorify us by obtaining the ranks in CHSE Odisha. Our motto is to enable the students efficient enough to grab the opportunity’s in this competitive world.
